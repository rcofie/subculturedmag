Budget Beauty Report

SUBCULTUREDApril 5, 2016
Uncategorized1 Comment

Get it here

RIMMEL VS CLINIQUE

If you are familiar with Clinique’s Chubby Sticks then you know how useful it is to essentially have a lip balm with the colour payoff of a lipstick. So if you don’t own one, then it’s time you add some of them to your makeup collection! Or you could save yourself some money and get Rimmel’s Colour Rush Balms instead. The colour payoff of the balms is great and they are very moisturising.

Get it here

KYLIE JENNER LIPS

 With everyone’s obsession with the Kardashian sister’s lips it’s no wonder that everyone wants to know what lip liner she uses. Apparently it’s Whirl by MAC. Don’t worry though if you don’t want to splurge on that! There is always Nyx’s Mauve for you… long-lasting, pigmented and super affordable.

Get it here

THE NEW CLARISONIC

Everyone knows that washing your face with your hands is not the way to go anymore. Battery-powered devices like the Clarisonic face brush do a much better job of cleansing and exfoliating your face. Alternatively, Olay’s Professional Pro-X Advanced Cleansing System comes with a face brush with a removable head and a complimentary facial cleanser, all at a consumer-friendly price. Time to update your cleansing routine!

Get it here

THE PERFECT RED

When seeking products for the perfect red lip, many gravitate towards MAC’s Rubby Woo or Russian Red. But you should give Rimmel’s Kate Matte lipstick in Kiss of Life a try. Very pigmented and long lasting and, even though it is a matte formula, it won’t dry out your lips. With this one great quality comes at a great price.

Text by Elpida Komianou
